About Me

I am a student at Becker College currently working towards my BA in Interactive Media Design, Computer Game Design Concentration. I have loved playing video games my whole life and now I love designing and creating them. My favorite video game genre would have to be MMORPG. I love the social aspect of the game and the massive sandbox world that you play in. I love creating colorful and fun levels and have been trying to perfect my craft everyday. The world is such an important thing in most video games and I want to be the one creating an awesome experience for people to enjoy.

Edgelord is a dungeon crawling RPG that I and a handful of others created in my Intermediate Game Development class. I was responsible for creating the levels for the game as well as coming up with the main goal of the game. In Edgelord, you play as a nameless character whose main goal is to find the portal within each level and escape.

Zoombies

Zoombies is a level based survival exercise game played in VR. This game was created in the Unity engine using the HTC Vive. I was an Assistant Design Lead for this project and was in charge of creating 3 of the 5 levels in the game. I worked in collaboration with 3 other designers. I created a forest, city, and graveyard level all in my vision. They all looked great and played smoothly. For this game we worked in collaboration with the company, Virzoom. We were able to make a game for them and they then are going to publish it.
